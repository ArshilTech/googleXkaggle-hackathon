      ℹ️ About Manayush

      🎯 Our Mission

      Manayush is a campus-tailored mental health platform designed to provide accessible, confidential psychological support for students in higher education. We believe every student deserves access to mental health resources in their language and context.

      ✨ Key Features

      • 🤖 AI-powered first-aid for immediate coping strategies
      • 📅 Confidential counsellor booking system
      • 🌐 Multi-language support (English & Hindi)
      • 📊 Anonymous analytics for campus wellness insights
      • 🤝 Moderated peer support community
      🔒 Privacy & Safety

      This is not a medical device. Manayush provides educational resources and first-aid guidance only. In emergencies, please contact your campus helpline or national emergency numbers immediately.

      All data is stored locally in your browser for this demo. No personal information is transmitted to external servers.

      🏆 Built for Students

      This prototype was developed to address the critical need for mental health support in educational institutions across India.
